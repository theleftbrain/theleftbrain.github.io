What the Great Lakes stand to lose from the Trump 2018 Budget.

@DomRussel - May 23, 2017

The 2018 Trump Administration Budget cuts all funding of the Great Lakes Restorative Initiative. As the map below shows, since 2010 the program has funded numerous projects to protect and improve the largest system of fresh water in the world. Last year the initiative received $300 million in funding. The Trump 2018 budget calls for a $1.6 billion down payment for new and replacement sections of a border wall between the U.S. and Mexico.


Data comes from the Great Lakes Restorative Initiative website. You can find my cleaned up Google Sheet used for this visualization here.
